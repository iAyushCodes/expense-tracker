Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes expense transactions (amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions), account credentials (email and password), and any notes or descriptions you add to your expenses. We do not collect sensitive financial information such as credit card numbers, bank account details, or social security numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of providing and improving the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating spending summaries and category breakdowns, and enabling you to filter and analyze your financial data. We do not use your personal financial data for advertising, marketing, or any purposes unrelated to the core functionality of the application.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard security measures. We implement encryption, access controls, and regular security audits to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal financial information to third parties. We may use trusted third-party services for hosting and infrastructure purposes, but these parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the purposes we specify. Our service does not integrate with external financial institutions or payment processors.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your data in Spendly. You can view, edit, and delete your expenses at any time through the application. If you choose to delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems. You may also export your data for personal use upon request.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session and provide a seamless experience. These cookies are essential for the functioning of the application and do not track your browsing behavior across other websites. We do not share cookie data with third-party analytics services.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of Spendly after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the application. We are committed to addressing your inquiries and ensuring your privacy is protected.
